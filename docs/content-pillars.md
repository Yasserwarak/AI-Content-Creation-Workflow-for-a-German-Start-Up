# Content Pillars

All content generated by the AI workflow must align with Steereon's five core content pillars.

1. Vacation, Free Time & Camping  
Outdoor adventures and leisure cycling.

Examples:
- bikepacking
- camping trips
- weekend cycling tours


2. Biking, Bike Technology & Design  
Content about bicycle engineering, innovation, and design.

Examples:
- e-bike technology
- bike components
- design features


3. Germany & Local Exploration  
Content focused on cycling and exploring Germany.

Examples:
- cycling routes
- German cities
- hidden destinations


4. Urban Mobility & Sustainable Transport  
Content about bicycles as a modern transportation solution.

Examples:
- commuting by bike
- sustainable mobility
- replacing cars with bikes


5. Cycling Lifestyle & Community  
Content about cycling culture and everyday riders.

Examples:
- rider stories
- cycling challenges
- community events
